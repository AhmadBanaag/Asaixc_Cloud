The Vision of this Gallery

I created this website with the purpose of reviving appreciation for Philippine classical paintings. While modern art has its own meaning and place in today's world, nothing can truly replace the beauty, talents, and mastery of Renaissance art. Through this gallery, I hope to help people rediscover how important Philippine paintings are, not only as historical treasures but as timeless reflections of Filipinos' emotion and creativity.

Spoliarium by Juan Luna

As one of the creator of this website, this is one of my favorite painting of all time. A monumental portrayal of human suffering and injustice. The fallen gladiators, stripped of dignity, mirror the struggles of a nation yearning for freedom and compassion beneath the shadows of oppression.

The Parisian Life by Juan Luna

A quiet yet unsettling glimpse into modernity and alienation. Beneath its café charm lies a reflection on identity, intimacy, and the silent distance between people in a changing world.

La Laguna Estigia by Felix Resurreccion

A haunting voyage across the mythic River Styx. Hidalgo captures the fragility of the soul in transition, a poetic meditation on death, redemption, and the timeless passage between worlds.
